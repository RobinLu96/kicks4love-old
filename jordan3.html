Design of Jordan III

Launched Date: May 7th, 1987

Retail Price:$175 USD

 

The Air Jordan III was originally released in 1988. It was the first Air Jordan designed by the legend, Tinker Hatfield. They debuted the iconic Jumpman logo we are all familiar with today. In addition, they were also the first Air Jordan to feature visible air and elephant print on the upper. After Air Jordan III releasing, Jordan series started to become one of the most popular sport series in the world

飛人喬丹III最初發布於1988年，是由傳奇人物，補鍋匠哈特菲爾德設計了第一飛人喬丹。他們推出標誌性的Jumpman標誌大家都熟悉的今天。此外，他們也是第一次飛人喬丹為特色可見的空氣和大象打印在上面。之後的Air Jordan III發布，喬丹系列開始成為最受歡迎的運動系列之一在世界上
