Buscemi golden lock

Launched Date: Fall/Winter, 2015

Retail Price:890 USD

 

Hand crafted in the Marche Region of Italy, the 100MM high-top sneaker is artfully constructed of full-grain Italian calf leather with hand painted edges and signature heel handle. The shoe is paired with gold plated hardware featuring a 3-piece closure and lock. 100 millimeters in height.

 

設計理念

發售日期: 2015年秋冬

發售價格: 890美金（大陸，港臺地區價格各有不同

Buscemi作為後起之秀，其經典的金鎖高幫鞋大受歡迎。 手工製作的意大利buscemi， 100mm的高幫運動鞋巧妙地構建了全粒意大利小牛皮手繪邊緣和簽名腳跟手柄。鞋是搭配鍍金硬件，採用3片關閉和鎖定。
