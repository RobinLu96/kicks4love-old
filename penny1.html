Penny I

Launched Year: 1996

Retail Price:$140 USD

 

Nike rewarded Penny, who seemed to fill the superstar void created in Air Jordan's absence, with his first official signature shoe for the 1995-96 season, the Air Penny. The Air Penny's most distinguishable and iconic design feature is its large wing-like panel.It also has a notable detail: the debut of Penny's 1 Cent logo on the heel.
