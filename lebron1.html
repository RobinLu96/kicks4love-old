Design OF LEBRON I

Launched Date: June 15th, 2003

Retail Price:$140 USD

 

The Nike Air Zoom Generation colloquially referred to as the LeBron 1 had a design inspiration based on LeBron James H2 Hummer. Only five colourways of the Nike Air Zoom Generation were released to the public.The full length Zoom Air, heel stabilizer, inner sock-liner, and Sphere material resulted in the Nike Air Zoom Generation being one of the most comfortable, well-ventilated shoes in the entire LeBron series.

Rookie

The Lebron I Rookie is for the first-year King James to play on the court with his own signiture shoes

勒布朗新秀是第一年的詹姆斯國王用他自己的簽名鞋在球場上打球

ASG stands for All Star Game; it is the shoes for memorize Lebron's first attendance in All Star game

ASG代表全明星賽，這雙鞋是為了回憶的勒布朗的第一次在全明星賽中出勤

FG

FG stands for First Game; it is the shoes to commemorates Lebron James's first NBA game
