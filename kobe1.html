NIKE ZOOM KOBE I. 耐克 科比一代.

Design OF KOBE 1

Launched Date: September 20th, 1991

Retail Price:$200 USD

 

Nike Basketball Designer Ken Link was assigned to create the first Kobe shoe. During the off-season, Kobe bulked up from two-a-day workouts and weighed in at 225 pounds; Link's goal was to create a basketball shoe that met the demands of a high-performing athlete like Kobe.

 

設計理念

發售日期: 2008年10月31日

發售價格: 140美金（大陸，港臺地區價格各有不同

發售日期: 1996年9月20日

發售價格: 200美金（大陸，港臺地區價格各有不同.

耐克籃球設計師Ken Link被分配到創建第一個Kobe鞋。在休賽期，科比膨體從兩一個天訓練，體重在225磅為鏈接的目標是建立能夠滿足一個高性能的運動員像科比的要求，籃球鞋。

Story oF KOBE I

  • 81 Points
  • PTS
  • Prelude
  • ASG

81 Points

Kobe scored 81 points playing against with Toronto Raptors. It is the second highest single game points in the NBA history. The shoes is to commemorate Kobe's incredible history.

科比拿下81分對陣多倫多猛龍隊。這是NBA歷史上第二場最高分單場紀錄。鞋子是為了紀念科比難以置信的歷史時刻。

PTS

The Kobe 1 PTS is to commemorate Kobe's first highest score in the season. It was 1998, only the third year of his career.

Kobe 1 PTS是為了紀念科比的第一次得分最高的賽季。那是1998年，他的職業生涯中唯一的第三年。

Prelude

The shoes is a created for the sheer respect to this young Kobe who is amazing on the court, after he earned his second championship.

這雙鞋是為純粹的對於這個年輕的科比誰是驚人的在球場上，他贏得了他的第二個總冠軍之後。

ASG

The footwear is to commemerate Kobe's first ALL STAR game. He was able to be voted to take part in the game even it was his first year of the career.

制鞋業是為了紀念科比的第一次全明星賽。他能夠被選為參加比賽，甚至是職業生涯的第一年。
