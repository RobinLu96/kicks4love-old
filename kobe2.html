Design OF KOBE II

Launched Date: September 20th, 1997

Retail Price:$200 USD

 

Nike Designer Ken Link was once again tapped to craft the Kobe II. The Kobe II was the first Nike hoops shoe to be completely stitched together instead of built with chemical bonding, which reduced waste and eliminated the use of toxic chemicals from the process. The midsole of the Kobe II was larger to provide lateral support and protection. Zoom Air? units were embedded in the insole on the heel and forefoot.
