Design Of LEBRON III

Launched Date: June 23rd, 2005

Retail Price:$140 USD

 

The overall design of the LeBron III resembles that of a high-tech army boot; the straps on the upper, micro-perforations on the leather, and sleek, linear midsole was more soldier than any LeBron shoe to date. And again, the large-volume Zoom Air unit returns. By the way, LeBron III had over 30 player edition.
