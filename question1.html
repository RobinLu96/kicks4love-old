Design of Question #1

Launched Date: July 3rd, 1996

Retail Price:$99 USD

 

Designed by Scott Hewett, the Question was pretty much a finished product before Allen Iverson even signed with Reebok. The toecap and huge Hexalite windows are recognizable anywhere, and the ghilly lace system didn't stray far from the primary shoe Allen wore at Georgetown the Air Jordan XI.

 

設計理念

發售日期: 1996年7月3日

發售價格: 99美金（大陸，港臺地區價格各有不同

由斯科特·休伊特設計，問題即使銳步簽約艾弗森之前成品。在包頭和巨大Hexalite窗戶可識別的任何地方，而ghilly鞋帶系統沒有從主鞋穿艾倫在喬治敦遠行於Air Jordan XI。

Comet

The most rare Question one, only remain 5 pairs in the world, designed to memorize the birth of Iverson's youngest daugther.

最稀有的問題一，世界上只剩5雙了，這設計是為了紀念艾佛森的最小的女兒

Rugby

AI was good at Rugby before entering NBA and used to be the Starting quarterback in his highschool. Rugby was designed to memorize his youth

艾倫艾佛森在Rugby之前進入了NBA，而他之前在高中當四分衛。Rugby是設計來紀念他的青春

Tiffany

The Tiffany is literally the linkage to the brand of Tiffany. It is the first association between Reebox and Tiffany

提芬妮是名義上和提芬妮這品牌的合作。這是銳步第一次和提芬妮合作打造鞋子
